OUR STORY
What began as an indulgence is now our passion.
At Minus 30, we strive to recreate what we grew up enjoying. Memories of
fun-filled decadence of our mothers’ home-made chocolate ice cream—fresh,
free of preservatives, healthy and full of love—inspired us to do the same.
We make irresistible gelato with the finest ingredients. For those that prefer it,
we have sugar-free and vegan options that are as good if not better than their
counterparts— you have to TASTE IT TO LOVE IT!
